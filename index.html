About Topview Hotel

Your Premier Destination
in Asaba

Located in the heart of Asaba's GRA Phase I, Topview Hotel is the business traveller's definition of comfort. We are within 10 minutes drive from Asaba International Airport and the Delta State Government House.


Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, our 57 tastefully furnished rooms and suites offer a perfect blend of contemporary luxury and warm Nigerian hospitality.
